What is psychological therapy?

Psychological therapy is a service provided by psychologists to provide a space where the patient can express their problems and the doctor can help them understand how both their emotions and their actions work in certain situations.
The goal of therapy is not only to provide the patient with a solution to her problems, but also for him to have the tools to avoid finding himself in that situation repeatedly.
